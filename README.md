## üéØ The Problem We Solve

**Traditional B2B payments are broken.** Vendors demand cash. Credit cards earn rewards but can't pay vendors. Bank transfers are slow and trackless.

**InvoicePe fixes this.** Pay any vendor with your credit card ‚Üí instant bank transfer to them. Get rewards. Keep records. Zero vendor onboarding required.
